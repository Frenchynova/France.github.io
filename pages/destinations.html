Choose by travel mood

Use pace, atmosphere, and trip length together

Most travellers decide faster once the destination is grouped by how the trip actually feels rather than by country alone.

Slow and scenic

Santorini or Maldives

Best when the holiday should feel restorative, polished, and naturally photogenic without needing a packed plan.

Social and high-energy

Dubai or Marrakech

Best when the traveller wants movement, city atmosphere, restaurants, and a destination with stronger day-to-night contrast.

Balanced and longer-stay

Bali or Amalfi Coast

Best when the traveller wants scenery and atmosphere, but still enough variety to fill almost a full week well.
