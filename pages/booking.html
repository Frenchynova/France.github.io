Before you fill the form

Three checks that usually make the enquiry better

A stronger enquiry usually comes down to three things: choosing the right pace, choosing the right room style, and being clear about what kind of trip this actually is.

Trip pace

Do you want movement or stillness?

Dubai and Marrakech suit busier itineraries. Santorini, Maldives, and Bali work better when the traveller wants the day to feel lighter.

Room priority

Is the room part of the experience?

If sea views, pool villas, or upgraded suites really matter, it is worth using the package tier and room type together before sending the form.

Reason for travel

Celebration, rest, or a sharper city break?

The more clearly the trip reason is stated in the notes, the easier it is to shape the enquiry around the right stay style.
