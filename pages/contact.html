Choose the right route

Use booking when you are ready. Use contact when you still need direction.

Both pages belong in the same journey. The booking page is best when the destination is mostly chosen. The contact page is better when the traveller still needs help narrowing the trip.

Use booking

You already know the destination

Best when the traveller can already choose the place, dates, room style, and trip priority.

Best when the traveller wants help with fit, pace, budget feel, or whether the stay should be slower, sharper, or more scenic.
