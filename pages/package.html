Before you choose

How the package system is meant to be used

Use the destination cards for quick browsing, the comparison table for pace and trip style, and the package builder when you already know which tier suits the trip.

Essential

A cleaner starting point for travellers who want the destination first and a simpler package structure.

Signature

The balanced middle tier for most travellers, with a stronger fit between comfort, planning detail, and budget.

Luxe

The highest tier for travellers looking for more room upgrades, stronger inclusions, and a more polished stay style.

How the tiers work

The destination stays the same. The service level changes the feeling of the trip.

Holidae packages are structured so travellers can choose the version of the destination that actually suits them. Essential keeps the enquiry light, Signature balances comfort and planning detail, and Luxe adds stronger room and stay preferences.

EssentialBest when the destination matters most and the stay can stay simple.
SignatureThe most balanced option for couples, first-time travellers, and most seven-night stays.
LuxeFor celebration trips, upgraded rooms, and a stronger premium finish.

Choose by occasion

Packages are easier to choose when the trip reason is clear

Travellers rarely choose a destination in isolation. They are usually choosing for a honeymoon, a shorter luxury break, a scenic week away, or a first high-end holiday that still needs to feel manageable.

Celebration trip

Maldives Luxe or Santorini Luxe

Best when privacy, upgrade potential, and a slower premium atmosphere matter more than seeing everything in one trip.

Short premium reset

Dubai Signature or Marrakech Essential

Best when the traveller wants a stronger sense of place over a shorter number of nights without overcomplicating the enquiry.

Week-long scenic stay

Amalfi Coast Signature or Bali Signature

Best when the traveller wants rhythm, atmosphere, and enough time to enjoy the destination without the itinerary feeling packed.
