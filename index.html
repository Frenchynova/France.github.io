Holiday styles

Start with how the trip should feel

The right destination comes from understanding the mood first. These four categories cover most of what travellers are actually looking for when they start planning.

Romantic escapes

Scenic settings, slower evenings, and destinations where the atmosphere does the work. Santorini and the Maldives both belong here.

Premium city breaks

Better hotels, better restaurants, and a destination that genuinely rewards the shorter trip. Dubai is the clearest example in the collection.

Beach and resort stays

The trip where the sunlounger is part of the point. The Maldives and Bali both deliver on this without asking you to work for it.

Coastal culture

Mediterranean and North African destinations where the town, the food, and the setting matter as much as the view. Amalfi and Marrakech.
